CONTEXT & CURRENT SITUATION:
----------------------------
We have successfully implemented the scroll-synced particles background, custom scroll scrubbing Lottie integration, and a first-time visitor virus attack experience with full browser autoplay audio compatibility.

Key Technical Achievements:
1. Removal of Click-to-Enter Cover Overlay (Start Directly on Primary Page):
   - The cover screen overlay was removed entirely. The site now opens directly onto the primary page without any pre-click cover.
   
2. Mouse-Move Triggered Virus Attack:
   - On the very first mouse movement on the normal page, the virus attack is triggered.
   - It retains the 1-second transition freeze delay (locking pointer-events, selection, and overflow on `body` via `.virus-lock` class).
   - After the 1-second delay, the page hides the normal site (`#app-container`) and shows the virus attack container (`#virus-attack-container`).

3. Foreground Corner Texts & Text Positioning in Virus Attack:
   - Inside the `#virus-attack-container`, two light grey (`#cacaca`) texts are styled in the `W95FA` font:
     * Top-Left: "click to hear the sound" (passive text with `cursor: default`, no click event handler. It automatically dims to 0.5 opacity once audio begins playing via user interaction).
     * Top-Right: "log in" (clickable text with `cursor: pointer`, currently triggers no action).
   - These are positioned in the foreground (with a high `z-index` of `100000`) so they are visible on top of both the cascade windows and the typing text phases of the virus attack.
   - **Padding Gutter (Overlap Prevention)**: Updated `.typing-container` in `style.css` to use `padding: 100px 40px 40px;` (from `20px`). This moves the simulated typing text and the question block down past the headers, keeping them clean and preventing any overlap.

4. First-Time Visit Virus Attack Logic:
   - Ported the React-based experience from the `virus_attack` directory to clean, vanilla ES module JavaScript in `virus.js`.
   - Copied assets (`0.jpg` through `5.png`) and the custom font (`W95F.otf`) from `virus_attack/public` into the main `public` directory.
   - **In-Memory Completion Status (Reset on Refresh)**: Changed the completion flag from `localStorage` to a module-scoped in-memory variable (`virusAttackCompleted`). This ensures that refreshing or reloading the browser page always resets the virus attack status completely, while answering the question still lets the user browse the normal site during their active session.
   - **Font Scaling (-40%)**: Reduced font-size of W95FA in the virus attack section by 40% (window title headers down to 7px, typing text down to 1.5vh-2.1vh).
   - Integrated the question validation: upon pressing Enter in the input state, the app sets `virusAttackCompleted` to `true`, removes the virus overlay, and displays the main website.

5. Lottie Scrubbing & Scroll Locking:
   - Replaced concentric SVG orbits with the "circular.input.json" Lottie animation.
   - Handled scale-aware viewport lock at "targetScrollY" with smooth entrance and exit glides.
   - Deceleration scrubbing (lerping with 0.06 factor) and adjusted sensitivity (0.00025) for high visual harmony.
   - Jitter-free native scroll locking by setting "overflow: hidden" on html.

6. Interactive Particles Background & Global Blending:
   - Enabled "#particles-bg-canvas" behind the "primary_page" content.
   - Set "#primary-page .canvas-container" background to "transparent".
   - **Global Particle Blending (Bypass Stacking Context Isolation)**: Because the `transform: scale` on `.canvas-container` isolates stacking contexts on smaller viewport widths, applying `mix-blend-mode: multiply;` directly on `.cosa-mi-consuma-img` would only multiply with its transparent parent container backdrop (rendering it solid white).
   - **Solution**: Changed `.particles-bg-canvas` style in `style.css` to `z-index: 1000;` and added `mix-blend-mode: multiply;` (and removed the local `mix-blend-mode` from `.cosa-mi-consuma-img`). Moving the canvas on top of the content layer and multiplying it globally allows the dark particle trails to draw on top of both the white page backgrounds and image borders dynamically, rendering them fully visible everywhere.
   - Adjusted Particle Trails Opacity: Reduced the opacity of the particles and stroke lines by 40%.
   - Shrunk Transparent Logo & Collision Mask: Scaled the transparent logo boundary down by 20%.
   - Restored Particle Trails: Changed canvas background fill style in the render loop from solid `#ffffff` back to `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`.
   - Synced opacity dynamically with the scroll bar:
     * Fades in from opacity 0 to 1 as scroll Y moves from 300px to 900px.
     * Fades out from opacity 1 to 0 as scroll position approaches the bottom order section.

 7. Image Remapping:
