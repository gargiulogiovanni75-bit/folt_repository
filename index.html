Noise. flesh. whisper. fragments. the skin becomes a screen, the mind a labyrinth of broken mirrors. listen? An irregular heartbeat, labored breathing, the echo of that which dares not speak to itself. There is no surface here. just falls, leaps into the void, sudden discards. fashion that scratches, art that screams silently, philosophy that slips into the folds of the soul. biting images, deep blacks with living grain, secrets Unveiled Between Shadows. You're a navigator of restless spaces, a seeker of burning details, a soul that wants to know, feel, expand beyond the confines of the skin. Music guides you, a wave that collapses and rebuilds, a psychological journey made of bright splinters and pulsating darkness. It is not a journey for those who fear chaos, for those looking for simple answers. it's a whirlwind, a tightrope dance, an exploration of the boundary between pleasure and annoyance, between desire and fear. Here you get lost to find yourself. here the raw becomes art, and art becomes life.

The Foundation FØLT is an independent editorial project founded in Naples in 2025. Conceived by Nicole Sciamanna, who serves as the project’s Founder and Creative Director, the magazine emerged as a technical and artistic response to a contemporary landscape of sterilized and over-polished content. It was established to provide a dedicated spaceboth physical and digital for creative expression that is no longer held by conventional restraints or industrial filters.
The Collective and Methodology the content within FØLT is produced by a collective of young artists driven by a collaborative and unfiltered approach. The editorial process is centered on a specific creative input or theme provided by the creative direction. In response, each artist develops an original output based entirely on their personal vision. This methodology ensures that every work is intrinsically charged with meaning, offering a raw and direct insight into the artistic reality of its creator without the interference of external "style guides" or aesthetic boundaries.
The Mission The mission of FØLT is to foster authentic artistic interpretation and provoke significant emotional reactions. By eliminating traditional editorial fences, the project creates a direct connection between the artist’s vision and the observer
